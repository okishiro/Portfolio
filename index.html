Kokuho

9/10, A masterpiece at the very least, it has a very non-contrived story with no go-to villan which overall feels very real and human. The art of kabuki is not for me yet, but this movie is exactly what I look for nowadays. I also loved the perfect amount of music each scene has.

Funny Games (1997)

8.1/10, My type of horror. A reminder for me what humans can be. simle and terryfying.

8.5/10, probably the best fun teenage movie ever made. Very iconic and very deserving.

7.5/10, I miss the editing and the crazy action of Fury Road. Interesting Characters, fine-ish storyline. I Need to watch fury road again.

Mrityunjay

3/10, Below mediocre story, wants to be smart but simply isn't. Dialogues are poor, action shots are very poor, emotional scenes are very poor, a very perfunctory movie at best.

Tár

7.8/10, It demands your brain, it very thoughtfully skips some info for you to figure it yourself. Always carry a premonition vibe. Marginally depressing at core.

Three Months

8/10, How do they have Ellen Burstyn in this movie? Cute, sweet, funny, great songs, characters feel real, less serious ladybird in a way.

The Odyssey

8.3/10, Might appreciate it more in the future. Technically outstanding. It just dosent feel like a years long journey, more like months. Some casting misfires in my opinion. Ummm, this is the guy who blew up a plane, rotated a truck, published papers on how a black hole must look like, literally has a peeing time schedule on set for himself, and now choses to go woke while adaptating a mythical story. its not bad, just does not fit properly.

The Drama

7.6/10, Both the leads are carrying the movie, feels real. The Concept is intriguing and well executed.
